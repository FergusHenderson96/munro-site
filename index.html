What is a Munro

A Munro is a mountain in Scotland that is over 3000ft (914.4m).

There are also 227 Munro Top's which are summits which are over 3000ft but are considered to be a subsidiary top to a nearby Munro.


Any hills below 3000ft but abover 2500ft (762m) is called a Corbett. There are currently 222 Corbetts. Similar to Munros, there are also 455 Corbett Tops.
